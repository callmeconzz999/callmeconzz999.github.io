About

callmeconzz999 is an Indigenous artist creating music rooted in truth, resilience, and lived realities. Their work speaks on the ongoing crisis of MMIW/MMIM/MMIP, mental health struggles, addiction, and the complexities of relationships — both personal and generational.

Each song is meant to honor stories that have been ignored, silenced, or misunderstood, while offering connection and understanding to those walking similar paths.

Music & Message

The music is not just sound — it is testimony. It carries grief, survival, anger, healing, and hope.
